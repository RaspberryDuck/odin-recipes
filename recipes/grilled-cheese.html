Grilled Cheese

Description

Make a warm, cheesy, toasty grilled cheese at home with this recipe! All it takes is 10 minutes. Try this recipe to enjoy a delicious grilled cheese now.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • American cheese
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Heat a skillet to medium heat. Melt a pat of butter.
  2. Place one slice of bread on the skillet and top with cheese.
  3. Toast until golden. Then remove the slice from the skillet.
  4. Melt another pat of butter and place the other slice of bread on the skillet.
  5. Place the first toasted piece ontop of the slice and heat until bottom slice is golden brown.
  6. Remove grilled cheese from skillet. Enjoy the gooeyness!
