Mashed Potatoes

Description

Make fluffy and creamy mashed potatoes at home with this recipe! All it takes is 35 minutes. Try it today!

Ingredients

  • Medium-sized potatoes
  • Milk
  • Cream cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil water in a large pan. Lightly salt the water.
  2. Peel and cut up your potatoes into quarters.
  3. Boil potatoes until a fork can easily pierce through them.
  4. Drain potatoes and then mash them.
  5. Gradually add milk and cream cheese to desired taste and consistency.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
