French Fries

Description

Make crispy golden fluffy french fries at home with this recipe! All it takes is 15 minutes. Try it today!

Ingredients

  • Medium-sized potatoes
  • Vegtable, canola, or peanut oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Heat up vegtable, canola, or peanut oil.
  2. Cut up your potatoes. Pat your potatoes dry with a paper towel.
  3. Fry your potatoes until golden. Let them dry on a paper towel covered plate.
  4. Season with salt.
